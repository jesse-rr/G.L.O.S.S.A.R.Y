### Game Simple Explanation

_The higher you climb Babel, the less the world obeys rules and the more it obeys concepts. Your glossary ties those concepts to you, never lose your glossary._

---

### Game Flow

1. Choose a Covenant
2. Enter floor
3. Identify possible symbols
4. As you identify a symbol (click) -> Fight starts with enemies based of the symbol. (Turn based)

> 4.1.Requirements: Must identify at least one symbol to progress to next level. (Win 1 Fight) - More symbols = Easier run but longer.

> 4.2 Choices: __Run__ (Only up to floor 3-4), __Fight__, __Special ability__. 

5. Go up a staircase to next floor (Repeat up to 6 -> 7 floor)
6. On 7th floor, as you enter, you trigger the boss fight. (If you win - Ascend to godhood, If you lose, animation of you being thrown down to the pile on corpses on the bottom of the tower - end run)

---

### Special Stats
- __Scorched__: The enemy receives turn based damage, less resistance and healing is reduced.
- __Suppressed__: The enemy cannot use special intents and its current intent is dazed (% of losing turn).
- __Fated__: When a Fated enemy acts, it repeats its previous intent next turn

---

### Currencies
All currencies have their means of getting them, specific currencies are tied to choosen covenant:
- __Money__ (General currency): Killed Enemies
  - __Echoes__: Analizing symbols (Echoes of the past)
  - __Ashes__: Damage taken (Sufferege)
  - __Tributes__: Sacrified (Items, Relics, Knownledge)

### Covenants Mechanics
#### [Ouroborus] - "For smart players/For achieving higher % rate"
- __Theme__: recursion, memory, inevitability.
- __Core Mechanic__: Reversal / Recursion
  - Once per floor, you may rewind a combat turn
  - Once per run, you may redo a cleared floor, but: __Enemies gain a mutation__ Or __you lose a relic / item__
  - Cards that:
    - Return discarded cards
    - Repeat last action
    - Trigger “on death” effects twice
- __Glossary tie-in__:
  - Identifying symbols gives Echoes
  - Echoes = currency used to rewind / repeat
  - Religious symbols = stronger Echo generation (because belief = memory)

#### [Dragon] - "For mid-maxing players that make sure they dont lose”
- __Theme__: hierarchy, dominance, inevitability of power. 
- __Core Mechanic__: Control the Enemy
  - Enemies can be Suppressed: Delays their turn
  - Reduces intent strength
  - “Threat” meter:The more damage you deal without being hit, the more enemies weaken
  - Elite enemies may kneel (skip a phase) if you break their morale
- __Glossary tie-in__:
  - Gods and monarch symbols unlock: Commands | Stances
  - Enemy debuffs that feel authoritative
    - Nordic / Roman symbols = dominance boosts
    - Religious symbols = obedience mechanics

#### [Phoenix] - "For high-risk/high-reward/snowball type players - Speedrunners"
- __Theme__: suffering → transcendence.
- __Core Mechanic__: Death Is a Resource
  - You revive, but: Lose max HP | Gain permanent buffs
  - Cards that:
    - Get stronger when burned
    - Trigger when discarded or destroyed
    - “Ash” resource:Gained when taking damage | Spent for healing, buffs, revives
- __Glossary tie-in__:
  - Religious symbols unlock resurrection variants
    - Egyptian symbols → soul / afterlife buffs
    - Christian symbolism → sacrifice effects

### Floor Mechanics

- __Floors 1–2__: Mundane Reality
  - Normal dungeon
  - Symbols are decorative
  - Clicking them does nothing… yet

- __Floors 3–4__: Cultural Bleed
  - Runes, pantheons appear
  - Symbols start triggering passive effects
  - Enemies adopt myth traits

- __Floors 5–6__: Symbol Collapse
  - Symbols overlap
  - Gods contradict each other
  - Enemies are no longer “creatures” but ideas

- __Floor 7__: Inhuman Apex
  - Language fails
  - Enemies represent:
    - Authority
    - Memory
    - Sacrifice

- _Your chosen Patron directly alters the final fight_
