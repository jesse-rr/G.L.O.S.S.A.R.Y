#### Animation for descovery - Search
1. Initial Discovery (Player clicks the symbol)

Visual: The symbol glows faintly, perhaps with subtle pulsing or breathing light, drawing attention.

Effect: A small shimmer or particle trail radiates from the edges, giving the sense it’s alive or important.

Sound: A soft chime or mystical hum to signal “interaction available.”

2. Uncovering / Understanding

Visual:

The symbol rotates, expands, or “unfolds” in place like a mandala revealing its full shape.

Light rays or energy lines shoot outward briefly to suggest insight spreading.

Optional: subtle glyphs or runes appear around the symbol, as if the knowledge is being absorbed.

Effect: The background could slightly darken to focus attention on the symbol.

Sound: A higher-pitched chime or harp-like sparkle to emphasize revelation.

3. Reward / Knowledge Gained

Visual:

After the fight, the symbol could merge into the player character with a glowing pulse or burst, showing the stat boost.

Small floating numbers or icons could indicate which stat is increased.

Effect: A quick rising light streak or aura around the character, signaling empowerment.

Sound: A satisfying “power-up” tone, distinct from the discovery chime.

4. Optional Extras

Progression Glow: As the player climbs the tower, faint residual traces of the symbol’s glow could follow them, reinforcing that knowledge was gained.

Trail Effects: When moving to the fight, a spark trail or subtle energy line leads from the symbol to the enemies.

Interactive Feedback: If the player fails, the symbol flickers or dims rather than rewarding, visually reinforcing the need to succeed to gain knowledge.
